---
title: "TShock permission nodes reference"
description: "Complete reference for TShock's permission nodes, organized by category: tshock.admin, tshock.world, tshock.npc, tshock.tp, tshock.ignore, and more."
---

TShock uses a hierarchical permission node system to control what actions each player group may perform. Nodes follow the `tshock.<category>.<action>` naming convention and are assigned to groups via the `/group` command or directly in the TShock database. This page documents every built-in permission node, the behavior it controls, and the commands it unlocks.

<Note>
  Permission nodes are assigned to **groups**, not individual players. Use `/group addperm <groupname> <permission>` to grant a permission and `/group delperm <groupname> <permission>` to revoke one.
</Note>

| `tshock.ignore.paint` | Prevents you from being disabled by paint abuse detection. | None |
| `tshock.ignore.placetile` | Prevents your tile placements from being reverted by abuse detection. | None |
| `tshock.ignore.projectile` | Prevents you from being disabled by projectile abuse detection. | None |
| `tshock.ignore.removetile` | Prevents your tile removals from being reverted by abuse detection. | None |
